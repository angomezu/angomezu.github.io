Research

I focus on 3D perception and geometric deep learning, with an emphasis on learning from unstructured point clouds and designing robust evaluation pipelines. My work centers on extracting and leveraging geometric structure for semantic understanding in real-world 3D data. My most recent work involved the develop of a 3D LiDAR plant organ segmentation deep learning model in collaboration with Oak Ridge National Laboratory. I evaluated geometry-aware neural networks, engineered local geometric features, and designed data-centric evaluation protocols under class imbalance and occlusion. I am interested in extending geometric perception methods toward embodied AI and robotics settings, particularly where reliable 3D understanding is critical for downstream decision-making.
